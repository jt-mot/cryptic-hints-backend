Hidden Word Clue
"Wicked thing concealed by the villain" (4)
Definition: "wicked thing"
Technique: This is a hidden word clue. The word "concealed" tells you the answer is hiding inside other words.
Breakdown: Look inside "thE VILlain" — the answer is hidden within these letters.
Answer: EVIL — A wicked thing, hidden inside "thE VILlain".
Charade Clue
"Animal bound on stake" (8)
Definition: "animal"
Technique: This is a charade — the answer is built by chaining smaller words or parts together, one after another.
Breakdown: "stake" can mean a bet placed before a game. "Bound" means to run with long strides. Put the bet before the run…
Answer: ANTELOPE — ANTE (a stake / bet) + LOPE (to bound / run) = ANTELOPE, an animal.
